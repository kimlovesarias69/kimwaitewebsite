About

Strategic operations for venture-backed companies

Kimberly Waite Strategic Operations provides fractional CEO and COO leadership to venture-backed startups at Seed and Series A. We work with founders during critical operational moments—governance challenges, founder conflict, board coordination, investor relations, and organizational restructuring.

We step in when companies are stuck, whether conflict is slowing execution, decisions aren’t getting made, or founders need senior operational support without a full-time hire.

We work directly with founders, boards, and legal teams to establish clarity, align stakeholders, and maintain operational continuity while key decisions are resolved.

We work with a small number of companies at a time, by design.

Approach

Clarity, structure, forward motion

Companies rarely stall because of a lack of ideas. They stall when decisions aren’t getting made, roles are unclear, and tension goes unaddressed. We work with founders and stakeholders to resolve what’s blocking progress and move the company forward.

01

Reveal

Identify the real issues beneath the surface—misaligned incentives, unspoken conflict, structural weaknesses, and decisions that have been deferred too long.

02

Resolve

Create clarity around roles, authority, governance, and next steps so difficult conversations can lead to actual decisions.

03

Stabilize

Build the operational structure, communication cadence, and accountability systems needed for the company to move forward with coherence.
